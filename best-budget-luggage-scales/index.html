Best budget luggage scales

Updated June 2026Independently researchedNo paid placement.

Quick answer

The Travelon Digital Luggage Scale is our top pick for budget-conscious travelers, combining accuracy, a backlit display, and a compact build at a very fair price.

The GreaterGoods is a close runner-up for those who want a slightly lower price, while the PerfectPrime is the most popular model for its reliable hold function and clear readout.

Nobody wants to be that person frantically repacking at the airport counter to avoid an overweight-bag fee. A good digital luggage scale pays for itself after one trip, and the best budget options do the job just as well as pricier models. The trick is finding one that is accurate enough to trust, easy to read in dim light, and small enough to toss into a carry-on without thinking. When shopping on a budget, focus on three things: capacity (most scales handle up to 110 lb, which covers checked bags), a backlit display (hotel rooms and overhead bins are dark), and a solid hold function so the reading stays on screen after you lift the bag. Battery life and build quality vary, but you don’t need to spend a lot to get a scale that will last for years of occasional use.
