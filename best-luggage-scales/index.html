Quick answer

The Etekcity Digital Luggage Scale is our top pick for its reliable accuracy, backlit display, and thoughtful features at a reasonable price.

The Amazon Basics scale is perfect for budget-minded travelers, while the Ozeri offers premium build and the Travelon is ideal for packability.

A digital luggage scale is one of the cheapest ways to avoid surprise baggage fees and the stress of re-packing at the airport. These handheld devices let you weigh your suitcase before you leave home, so you can redistribute items or shift things to a carry-on. The best models are accurate, easy to read, and compact enough to stash in a pocket or toiletry bag. When shopping, look for a scale that can handle at least 50 pounds (the typical airline limit for checked bags), has a clear backlit display, and includes a tare function so you can weigh items in a bag or case. Battery life and build quality vary, but most run on standard coin-cell or AAA batteries. We focused on well-regarded models with strong owner feedback and consistent expert reviews to narrow the field.
